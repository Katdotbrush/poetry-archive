Warp Tunnel (Translations)

I. DARK NIGHT

The dark night comes in.
I was standing at the front of a café; looking from far away the rain.
I started walking, but not knowing to where — just looking to redeem.
Therefore though, the only think I could feel at that moment was:
Uncertainty, wandering in a world without hospitality but hostility.

II. BUSINESSMAN

The bussinessman walked on the narrow streets.
His body shook of fear; from head to feet.
His heart though, brave was — not giving a chance to getting out of the beat.
With clothes that were fit, just commonly Monday sweating.
What was it? — He didn't know.
But one thing though told him that he wasn't coward.
But, walking with the flow.

III. SWINGING MY SWORD

That's like those mercenaries on the dark alleys.
Turning quick their eyes and moving their hands over weird ways.
Swinging their sword, Japanese style.
Nose feeling smells in over a mile.
Not indentifying their face.
And when the foes rush in! They swing the sword, in pure chase.
And to the foes, tell them I'm the pure ace!

IV. THE OLD FIELDMAN

Over a green hill, there was an old fieldman.
He sat over the armchair; at the front of the cottage house.
With his tired eyes, he saw the pasture ran.
Smoking a pipe, gentle ember.
Knowing that, at the end of the sunset — he would become obscure
And none of his partners, to him wouldn't remember.
And such pain, the fieldman would endure.
